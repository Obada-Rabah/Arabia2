Comprehensive Family Law Services

At Al Arabia Firm, we offer experienced legal support in family law matters based on Syrian Personal Status Law, providing guidance and protection for you and your family.

Our Family Law Services Include:

  • Marriage and prenuptial agreements
  • Divorce proceedings and settlements
  • Child custody and visitation rights
  • Alimony and child support arrangements
  • Inheritance and wills in accordance with Syrian law
  • Legal guardianship and family disputes

Why Choose Al Arabia Firm?

Our team is deeply familiar with the Personal Status Law and Sharia-based regulations in Syria, ensuring your case is handled with sensitivity, expertise, and confidentiality.

We are committed to protecting your rights and achieving the best outcomes for you and your family through professional legal advice and effective representation in Syrian courts.
